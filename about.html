About Us

Meet the people behind JCRES

Linda Garcia

Broker / Owner

Linda Garcia is the driving force behind Jones & Company Realtors (JCRES), bringing nearly two decades of exceptional experience in Silicon Valley's dynamic real estate market. As a second-generation broker and a lifelong resident, her deep understanding of the local landscape, combined with an unwavering commitment to client success, has established her as one of the region's most trusted real estate professionals.

With a background that includes expertise in Commercial and Residential sales, Property Management, and Lending, Linda brings a unique and comprehensive perspective to every transaction. She understands that buying or selling a home is one of life's most significant decisions, and she approaches each client relationship with the dedication, integrity, and expertise it deserves.

Linda's approach is simple: listen to her clients' needs, provide honest guidance, and work tirelessly to exceed expectations. Her track record speaks for itself, with countless successful transactions and clients who return for her services time and again. She is more than just a broker; she is a deeply invested community member committed to the success and well-being of the Silicon Valley area.
